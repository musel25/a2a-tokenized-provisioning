## Surprises / deviations from the plan

- The §3.1 payloads became pydantic models in `a2a_interfaces` (rule 3 — they cross
  the consumer-agent↔controller boundary; ctrl-mcp consumes them at M5.4). The §3.1
  examples elide `"v"`; the §0 convention wins, as a validated default. No shape
  *changed*, so no `v` bump.
- Status mapping decided and recorded (docs/05 §6): 404 unknown · 401 proof failures
  (E_BAD_PROOF, E_NONCE_REUSED) · 403 ticket-says-no (owner/window/revoked/scope/
  conflict) · 502 E_NETWORK.
- `ControllerService` is the stub controller grown up: same ports, plus real auth,
  translator dispatch via `getattr` over `ProvisionerCall`s, failure-path sweep, and
  the two autonomous teardown hooks (`tick`, `handle_revoked`) that M4.5 wires to a
  timer and the Revoked watcher.
- Honest note: controller *tests* borrow `FakeChain` from e2e (the plan says "httpx
  tests against fakes"); the shipped package depends only on interfaces. If that test
  edge ever grates, the fakes could move to an `interfaces.testing` module — deferred.
