## Surprises / deviations from the plan

- **pygnmi's `skipverify` cannot connect to this lab at all** — it surfaces as a bare
  `FutureTimeoutError`, and only `GRPC_TRACE=handshaker` digging revealed the real
  error: `CERTIFICATE_VERIFY_FAILED: self signed certificate`. Two stacked causes:
  (1) the router presents a *self-signed leaf* (CN=srl1 — the containerlab CA never
  reaches the node; same sudoless-overlay breakage as the M2.1 startup-config), and
  (2) python-grpc verifies regardless. The recipe that works — trust-on-first-use:
  fetch the leaf with `ssl.get_server_certificate`, pass it as `path_root` (a
  self-signed cert is its own CA), `override="srl1"` (the cert's SAN, NOT the container
  hostname). gnmic never hit any of this because Go's skip-verify really skips.
- **The lab's `/etc/hosts` entries are IPv6-only** and python-grpc won't dial them;
  the smoke script resolves the node's IPv4 via `docker inspect`. gnmic (Go) dialed
  IPv6 happily, which is why M2.3 never noticed.
- Both gotchas recorded in docs/07's appendix; M3.2's provisioner will encapsulate the
  connection recipe so nothing downstream ever sees it.
